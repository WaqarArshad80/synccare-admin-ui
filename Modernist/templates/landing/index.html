What Takt does

01

The clockface timetable

Every service leaves at the same minute past every hour, all day, forever. Your riders stop reading timetables and start setting watches — the schedule becomes something the town simply knows.

02

Connections that hold

Transfers are planned as promises: the branch line waits the full window, the mainline meets it to the second, and a missed connection is treated as what it is — an engineering defect.

03

Disruption, rerouted

When the mountain sends snow, Takt already has the replacement plan published, the buses rolling, and the apology drafted in four languages. The 7:02 still leaves at 7:02 — from the next platform over.

The network

Printed in black and white
